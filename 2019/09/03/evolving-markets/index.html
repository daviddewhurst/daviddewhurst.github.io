Evolving markets?

2019-09-03
Tags: growing-systems.
Categories: None.

Financial markets are subject to competitive pressure in response to which agents can either adapt (in myriad ways) or die. Over longer timespans, species of agents are also subject to evolutionary pressure as entire classes of trading and wealth-management strategies interact and prove themselves successful or otherwise.

My colleague Colin Van Oort and I have some interest in this area. To this effect, we have written (and continue to write, albeit infrequently) an agent-based financial market model in which different classes of agents can compete against one another.
