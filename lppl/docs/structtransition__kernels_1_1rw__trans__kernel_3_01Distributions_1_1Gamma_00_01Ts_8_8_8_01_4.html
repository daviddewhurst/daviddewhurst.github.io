Detailed Description

template<typename... Ts>
struct transition_kernels::rw_trans_kernel< Distributions::Gamma, Ts... >

Specialization of rw_trans_kernel for gamma distributions.

The gamma distribution in \((k, \theta)\) is transformed to (location/scale) = \((\mu, \sigma)\) form by the following equations: \(k = (\mu/\sigma)^2;\ \theta = \sigma^2 / \mu\).
